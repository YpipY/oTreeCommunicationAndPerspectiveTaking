In the communication game, you will take turns being either the “Director” or the “Matcher.”


When you are the Director, you will be shown a target on the screen that you have to communicate to the Matcher by sending a signal.


When you are the Matcher, you will receive the signal that the Director sent, and your task is to select the item that you think the Director wanted to communicate, out of several possibilities on the screen.
